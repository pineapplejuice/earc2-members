EARC End-Fed Matchbox Antennas

In XXXX, a couple of hams in our fundraising committee and antenna group, Richard Hardy KH6HNL (sk) and Chuck Hanebuth KH7HNL devised an easily-built 9:1 UNUN toroid matching transformer that takes the convenience of an end-fed dipole and brings the SWR down to the range where an antenna tuner can properly tune the antenna.

This project can be done by any radio amateur who can get the necessary parts to assemble it, and we freely publish the assembly instructions on our site.
