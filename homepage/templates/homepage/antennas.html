EARC End-Fed Matchbox Antennas

In 2011, a couple of hams in our fundraising committee and antenna group, Richard Hardy KH6HNL (sk) and Chuck Hanebuth KH7HNL, devised an easily-built 9:1 UNUN toroid matching transformer that takes the convenience of an end-fed dipole and brings the SWR down to the range where an antenna tuner can properly tune the antenna.

This project can be done by any radio amateur who can get the necessary parts to assemble it, and we freely publish the assembly instructions on our site.

For those who don't want to assemble it, volunteers from our club will assemble the antenna for you and ship to you via U.S. Priority Mail with 30 feet on antenna wire for $56.00.

Sorry, we do not ship antennas outside of the United States.
