Amateur Radio Nets

EARC Nightly Net

Aloha! Please join us Monday to Saturday nights (except major holidays or EARC meeting days) for the EARC nightly net. All amateur radio operators of all abilities and experience levels are welcome, and we especially welcome newcomers to ham radio or to the islands.

To help new hams get accustomed to operating in a directed net environment in an emergency situation, we run our EARC nets as directed nets. We try at all times to keep our nets accessible and friendly.

Time: 7:30 p.m. HST

Frequencies: 146.880 (-), DEM/EARC linked repeater system

Net Control Stations (NCS)

Our Net Control Team is always looking for Net Control Stations (NCSes) to help run the net. Training is provided! Email our Net Manager, Joe Tabrah KH6FHI, for more details.
